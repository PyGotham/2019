Lisa Tagliaferri

Lisa Tagliaferri is on the Developer Educator team at DigitalOcean. A recovering medievalist with a background in the digital humanities, she has been a researcher and educator at MIT and the City University of New York. Her open-access eBook, How To Code in Python, is widely used to teach Python.
